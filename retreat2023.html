DaST Research Retreat

Locarno, 12 - 15 April 2023

Research Retreat 2023

Agenda

Wednesday

Before 8 pm: Arrival at the hotel Belvedere in Locarno
8 pm: Dinner at the hotel

Thursday

9 am - 1 pm:
  • Sachin Basil John (EPFL): Interactive Speed Querying on High-Dimensional Data with Partially Materialized Data Cubes
  • Tian Zilu (EPFL): Generalizing Bulk-Synchronous Parallel Processing for Data Science: From Data to Threads and Agent-Based Simulations
  • Christoph Mayer (University of Zurich): Reinforcement Learning for Factorized Databases
  • Moe Kayali (University of Washington): Quasi-stable Coloring for Graph Compression
1:30 pm: Lunch at the hotel
3 pm - 6 pm: Walk to Ascona

Friday

8:00 am: Hike in Valle Verzasca
19:00 pm: Dinner at restaurant Blu in Locarno

Saturday

9 am - 9:45 am:
  • Nils Vortmeier (Ruhr-University): Teaching reductions: Formal foundations
  • Peter Lindner (EPFL): Probabilistic Query Evaluation with Bag Semantics
  • Moe Kayali (University of Washington): Unifying Data Discovery and Exploration with Foundation Models
  • Anton Lykov (University of Washington): ExpressQuery: Query Answering with Knowledge Graphs
  • Niko Göbel (RelationalAI): Challenges of putting IVM into production
  • Ahmet Kara (University of Zurich): Ranking Database Facts by their Shapley Values
1:30 pm: Lunch at the hotel
3:00 pm: Closing
